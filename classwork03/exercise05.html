School of Art and Science Information Portal

This website helps students stay informed about assignments, exams, timetables, and more.

Advising the School

To maintain quality control over web design, the school should create a central stylesheet that all instructors follow. This ensures consistency across pages, prevents clashing styles, and makes updates easier.

Explaining the Process

Instead of each teacher writing their own styles, the school should adopt a single external CSS file. Teachers can then focus on updating content only, while the design remains uniform. This keeps the site professional, accessible, and easier to maintain.

Exploring HTML Tags and Styles

Examples of useful tags and styles:

  • <h1>, <h2>, <p> — for headings and text
  • <div> — to group sections
  • <table> — for timetables
  • CSS styles like color, font-size, margin, padding
